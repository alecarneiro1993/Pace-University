About me

Like I said in the Home page, my name is Alexandre Carneiro, I'm an exchange student from the Brazil Scientific Mobility Program, a governmental sponsored program that encourages Brazilian students to study and learn from universities from around the world.

I come from Rio de Janeiro, also known as "Cidade Maravilhosa" or "Marvellous City" in English.
The University I go to is called "Universidade Veiga de Almeida" (Veiga de Almeida University) and my major there is Computer Engineering. I was in my 8th term when I decided to take the exchange program. The reason for that was to improve my language skills, programming skills and professional career.

In my whole life, I've worked for two different companies as an intern:
The first one was in an insurance company called "Sulamerica Seguros" in which I was a Software Engineering intern.
The second was in my father's company which is called "RC Ambiental", an environmental legislation company, where I worked as a Web Developer intern.

My hobbies are playing video games, guitar, studying and working out.
